2016/17 Entertainment Books for Your Children

Kids Tree Preschool is excited to be joining in the Entertainment Membership Fundraising initiative again this year. We will be selling both the traditional Entertainment Book Membership that comes with the Gold Card and vouchers and the Entertainment Digital Membership that puts the value of the Entertainment Book into your iPhone or Android smartphone. You will be able to choose an entertainment membership that’s the perfect fit for you, with the option of having the good old Entertainment Book or the new Digital Membership on your smartphone. The Digital Membership puts the value of the traditional Entertainment Book into your hand, and allows you to access it on the go and share with another family member.

Last year’s 2015/16 edition was an amazing success with more books than ever being sold, all thanks to our supporters who have been outstanding and done a stella job with taking it on to be our local re-sellers. All Entertainment™ Memberships described above contain over $20,000 in valuable offers you can use until 1st June, 2017 including thousands of valuable up to 50% off and 2-for-1 offers from many of the best restaurants, cafés, arts, attractions, hotel accommodation, travel and much more! Memberships are available for sale now, either through our website or on the order form that will be sent home during the following week. If you purchase your membership before August 21, you will also receive over $200 worth of Bonus Offers which you can use straight away!
